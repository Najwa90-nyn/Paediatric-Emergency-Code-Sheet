Welcome to Paediatric Emergency Code Sheet

Paediatric emergencies require precise and timely management. This tool provides an automated calculation of essential parameters, including endotracheal tube sizes, defibrillation and cardioversion joules, and resuscitation medication dosages. By entering the age and weight of the patient, the tool generates accurate, evidence-based results tailored to the patient's needs. This ensures a streamlined approach during critical situations, reducing errors and enhancing patient safety.
